Secure Crypt is an encryption tool that allows you to encrypt and decrypt files and folders directly in your browser. It's portable, meaning no installation required.

Here's how it works:

1Once you launch your SecureCrypt.exe, SecureCrypt will start a WSGI server that runs completely in your computer. This server will immediately bring up http://127.0.0.1:5000/ which will bring you to an activation page.

4This is my example.docx file we will be encrypting and decrypting. We're going to head back to our webpage and click the 'Encrypt a File' button seen above.

7Once you've made a strong password, click 'Encrypt & Download'. Instructions for how to edit your encrypted file name are under the 'Encrypt & Download ' button. It's important you read those instructions if you plan to edit the name of your file.
